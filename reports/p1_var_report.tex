\begin{abstract}
This report documents a multi-asset risk engine implementing three Value-at-Risk
methods (Historical Simulation, Parametric, Monte Carlo) and Expected Shortfall,
with a full backtesting suite and stress testing module. The engine is aligned
with FRTB (BCBS 352/457) regulatory standards including ES at 97.5\% and the
dual backtesting framework.
\end{abstract}
